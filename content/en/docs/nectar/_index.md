# Nectar - Personal AI, Powered by STING

Nectar brings the power of STING to your desktop. Everything runs locally on your machine.

## What is Nectar?

**Nectar** is the desktop edition of STING designed for:
- Individual users
- Privacy-focused users who want 100% local AI
- Anyone evaluating STING before deploying STING Server for their team

## Nectar vs STING Server

| Feature | Nectar (Desktop) | STING Server |
|---------|------------------|---------------|
| **Target User** | Individuals | Teams |
| **Installation** | Desktop app (manages Docker) | Docker Compose |
| **Users** | Single user | Multi-user |
